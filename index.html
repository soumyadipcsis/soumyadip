About Me

Hello! I am Soumyadip Bandyopadhyay, currently a Scientist at ABB Corporate Research Lab. My work focuses on verification of PLC programs, validation of Generative AI outputs, and forward engineering using LLMs. At ABB, I blend engineering excellence with innovation.

I earned my Ph.D. from IIT Kharagpur, specializing in Formal Methods and Software Engineering. My doctoral journey sparked a passion for rigorous system design and advanced development techniques.

I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Hasso Plattner Institute in Germany, followed by Assistant Professor at BITS Pilani, Goa, and later, Senior Formal Verification Engineer at NVIDIA.

I’m also passionate about world politics, folk music, and writing poetry.
